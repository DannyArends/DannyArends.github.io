Danny Arends is a PHD student Bioinformatics at the Groningen Bioinformatics centre (GBIC) part of the University of Groningen. Also part-time available at the hospital at the Genomics Coordination Centre (GCC).

Danny develops new methodologies and creates (open source) software which are used in research. The focus of his PHD thesis is computational methods in quantitative trait loci (QTL) analysis, and integration in a useful an comprehensive manner.
